ULTIMATE SPACE INVADERS

Instructions

Controls:

  • Move Left: ← or A
  • Move Right: → or D
  • Shoot: Space
  • Switch Weapon: 1-5 (number keys)
  • Pause: ESC or P

Gameplay:

  • Defeat all enemies in each level
  • Collect gold from defeated enemies
  • Gain a random skill after each level
  • Buy and upgrade weapons in the shop
  • Combine 3 identical weapons to upgrade to next tier

Weapons

  • Gun: Balanced damage, speed and price
  • Machine Gun: Fast shooting, less damage
  • Shotgun: Multi-projectile, medium damage
  • Rocket: High damage, slow reload
  • Laser: Piercing beam, high price
  • Sniper: Piercing shot, high damage, slow reload
  • Flamethrower: Burns enemies over time
  • Railgun: Pierces all, electric shock
  • Plasma Cannon: Huge plasma ball, splash damage
  • Ice Blaster: Slows enemies
  • Toxic Sprayer: Poisons enemies

Weapon Tiers

  • Common: Base stats (white)
  • Uncommon: 1.5× stats (green)
  • Rare: 2× stats (blue)
  • Epic: 3× stats (purple)
  • Legendary: 6× stats (gold)
  • Mythic: 8× stats (pink)
  • Exotic: 10× stats (cyan)
  • Transcendent: 20× stats (red)
